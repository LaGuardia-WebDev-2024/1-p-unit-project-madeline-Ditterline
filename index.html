What is Twin Peaks?

Twin Peaks is a TV show that was crated by David Lynch and Mark Frost. The show started airing in 1990 and finished out its second season in 1991. In 2017, the showrunners aired a third season with many of the same characters as a sequel to the original run of the series.
Though the show started out as a surrealist satire of the soap operas of the time, the emotional arcs, comedy, drama and horror made the show truly iconic. Many things that weren't very common in TV shows at the time have become a staple of our modern TV culture. Lynch and Frost mastered tonal whiplash within this series, being able to go from a light-hearted comedic scene to discussing a horrific murder in just a matter of moments.

The cast of Twin Peaks

Twin Peaks contains many different storylines and many different characters.
  1. The Palmer Family
    • Laura Palmer: Laura Palmer was a seventeen year old with a tragic life and many dark secrets. The discovery of her body and the investigation of her murder sets the entire show in motion.
